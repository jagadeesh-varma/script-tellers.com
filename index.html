About Us

Mahi Illindra is a filmmaker and a writer was born and raised in Ongole, Andhra pradesh, India. He studied Engineering at Bangalore. He began work in the film industry as a script Apprentice for Uyyala jumpala (2013), later became a writer for Venkatadri express (2013), Express Raja (2015) and Chalo (2018). Mahi Illindra founded a production house named 'Script Tellers" and started his web series production journey with Yupp TV. He wrote and produced his first web series for Yupp TV named 'Hey krishna' (2019) , post that My Marapu for Sony Liv (2019), They Call him Animal (2020), Kotha Poradu (2020) for Aha, Hooked (2021) and Bad trip (2021) for Sony Liv. Mahi Illindra's series have been broadcast internationally on YuppTv, Sony Liv and Aha.
